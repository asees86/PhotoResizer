Kerala PSC photograph rules

  • Exactly 150 × 200 pixels, JPG format, 30 KB or less
  • Name and date of photo printed in black on a white strip at the bottom (this tool adds it)
  • Taken within the last 6 months
  • Light / white background; face, both ears and shoulders clearly visible
  • No caps, dark glasses or heavy shadows

Kerala PSC signature rules

  • Signature only — no other text or marks in the image
