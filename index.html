Kerala PSC photograph rules (official PDF)

  • Exactly 150 × 200 pixels, JPG format, 30 KB or less
  • Candidate's name and date of photo printed on the photograph — mandatory (this tool adds it)
  • The photo must have been taken within the last 6 months
  • Colour or black & white; face and shoulders clearly visible
  • White or light-coloured background
  • Face straight and fully centered; eyes clearly visible
  • No caps or goggles (religious headwear allowed); no side-profile or unclear face
